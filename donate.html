Help Improve Our Chapter!

Why donate to our chapter?

We instill the "Better Men. Better Lives" mindset.

Our Chapter embodies the "Better Men. Better Lives." experience. We believe that in the development of our brothers so that they obtain the skills that they need to become an active member in their community. Our goal is to have men enter our chapter with the mindset to become someone better; and leave having accomplished that goal as well as have the motivation to take the next steps of their lives head on.

Our chapter currently offers several ways to improve the lives of our brotherhood. Brothers are able to take on leadership positions which give them the opportunity to make changes happen and make this chapter their own. It also gives brothers the opportunity to make their own ideas come to life as the brotherhood gets behind them. After all, a single person can formulate the idea. But it takes a community, such as our chapter, to make the idea come to life. With the help of our National Organization, brothers can potentially make a global change which would make an impact on our world.

We strive for improvement.

Gamma Chi consistently strives to improve. We raise our flag high because we are proud of our prior accomplishments as well as striving towards completing more of our goals. It took hard work and dedication to get us where we were. We do not want to let our alumni down and waste the foundation that was build up by our previous classes. Building upon this foundation sets our traditions and only sets us up for improvement. Every brother that walks through our doors sees who we are. Everyone thinks differently. It is up to our New Members to bring their ideas in and build upon them up until the time they leave.



We Host Educational Seminars.

Every month, our Chapter hosts educational seminars and Academic based dinners to help improve our members. These seminars consist of teachers, faculty, and alumni that come and speak to the current undergraduates to offer advice, guidance, and wisdom. The speakers share experiences in their lives that they think would be meaningful for us to learn about. These events not only help out our brotherhood, but helps connect students with faculty, and faculty with students which makes our community closer.

We give back to our community.

We are devoted to giving back to our community. Every year we part take in various community service events such as the Philly Marathon and the Philly Cleanup. We believe that hard work will help make Philadelphia a better place to live. Just last year, our chapter recorded 2169 hours of community service with an average of 30.99 hours per brother!

Some other events that our chapter participates in are the following:

  • Event Security for the Eagles
  • Red Cross Blood Drives
  • CCI Peer Mentorship
  • Student Ambassador
  • Freshment Move-In
  • Race for Hope
  • Various events for State Senators
  • And many more!

We care.

We care about our community and the people that decide to join our organization. Your donation helps us in numerous ways:

  • Leadership programs
  • Philanthropy
  • Chapter Advancement
  • Brother Advancement
Your donation will make a positive impact in brother's lives. If you would like to learn more, then please email our Recruitment Chair. He can clarify any and all questions and concerns that you may have.
